Our Story

OUR STORY

1938

Miramar Food Hall was born from a vision to bring new life to one of San Clemente's most treasured landmarks. Once home to the Miramar Theatre and the San Clemente Bowling Center, this 1938 Spanish Colonial Revival gem had been closed for over three decades.

2020

In 2020, we took on the challenge of restoring the space, honoring its rich history while transforming it into a vibrant culinary destination. Today, Miramar Food Hall stands as a celebration of community, global bites, local vibes and the enduring spirit of Southern California, marking the long-awaited revival of an iconic coastal landmark.

2026

Rooted in the relaxed spirit of coastal California, Miramar Food Hall brings together the artistry of local chefs and global flavors under one roof. Designed as a family-friendly gathering place, we offer an elevated yet approachable dining experience that reflects a balance of tradition and innovation, celebrating the region's diverse culinary scene while honoring the charm and character of this historic setting.
